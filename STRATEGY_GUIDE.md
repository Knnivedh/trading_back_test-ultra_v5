## 2. THE STRATEGY (How it works)

We don't rely on luck. We rely on **"Confluence"** (multiple things agreeing) and **"AI Validation"**.

### A. The 6 Indicators (The "Engine")
We look at 6 different market signals. We need **at least 3** to agree to consider a trade.

1.  **EMA Trend (20/50/200):** Are prices moving up or down? (Trend direction)
2.  **Supertrend:** A popular indicator that simply says "Buy" or "Sell".
3.  **VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price):** Where are the big institutions buying?
4.  **Bollinger Bands:** Is the price cheap (low) or expensive (high)?
5.  **ADX (Trend Strength):** Is the trend strong (>20) or weak?
6.  **StochRSI:** Is the market overbought or oversold?

**Rule:** If **3 out of 6** say "BUY", we have a potential trade.

### B. The AI Gatekeeper (The "Brain")
Once the indicators say "Go", we ask the AI (Llama-3.3-70B).
We send the chart data to the AI and ask: *"Rate this trade from 0 to 10."*

*   **Score < 7.0:** **REJECT.** (Too risky, ignore it).
*   **Score 7.0 - 7.9:** **Standard Trade.** (Risk 1.5% - 3.0%).
*   **Score 8.0 - 8.9:** **Strong Trade.** (Risk 4.5%).
*   **Score 9.0+:** **SNIPER TRADE.** (Risk 6.0% - Go big).

### C. Risk Management (The "Safety Belt")
This is how we survived and grew fast.
*   **Stop Loss (SL):** We always set a safety exit. If price moves against us, we exit small.
*   **Take Profit (TP):** We have 3 targets.
    *   TP1: Secure some profit.
    *   TP2: The main target.
    *   TP3: For massive trends.
*   **Aggressive Scaling:** When we win, our account grows. The next trade risks a % of the *new, larger* balance. This is "Compound Interest" on steroids.
